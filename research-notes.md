## Vast.ai Pricing

**Source URL:** https://cloud.vast.ai/
**Date:** Feb 6, 2025

**Marketplace pricing (varies by provider):**

Consumer GPUs:

- RTX 4090 (24GB): $0.29-0.78/hr (typical: ~$0.60/hr)
- RTX 3090 (24GB): $0.40-0.60/hr (typical: ~$0.50/hr)
- RTX 5090 (32GB): $0.38-1.08/hr (typical: ~$0.70/hr)

Datacenter GPUs:

- A100 (80GB): $0.84-1.49/hr (typical: ~$1.20/hr)
- H100 (80GB): $1.47-2.94/hr (typical: ~$2.50/hr)
- H200 (140GB): $2.07-5.07/hr (typical: ~$4.00/hr)

**Notes:**

- P2P marketplace - prices fluctuate based on availability
- Lower prices = less reliable providers
- Higher prices = datacenter/verified providers
- Reliability varies (98%-99.9%)
